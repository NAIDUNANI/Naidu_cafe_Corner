CAPPUCCINO

Cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with steamed milk including a layer of milk foam.

ESPRESSO

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee produced by forcing hot water under high pressure through finely-ground coffee beans.

LATTE

Caffè latte, often shortened to just latte in English, is a coffee drink of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk.

IRISH

Irish coffee is a caffeinated alcoholic drink consisting of Irish whiskey, hot coffee and sugar, which has been stirred and topped with cream.
